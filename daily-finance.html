Financial Technology

Daily Finance

Breaking down personal finance into daily income and expenses for clearer money management.

The idea is to break finance into smaller pieces — daily income and daily expenses — so individuals clearly see their financial spread.

Changes to Earnings

Allow daily payroll payments. Salaried employees are straightforward; hourly workers need a 1-day delay for manager confirmation.

Changes to Expenses

Monthly lenders allow daily payment processing. Variable expenses are amortized over 30 days. Example: a $3.00 purchase = $0.10/day for 30 days.

Where to Start

Expense Side: Leverage Apple Card as the starting point. Apple could offer checking/savings and coordinate daily payments to lenders.

Income Side: Financial institutions process daily payroll electronically. Bonuses spread over time. Commissions paid over the following month.
