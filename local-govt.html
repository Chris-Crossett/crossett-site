Local Reform

Local Government

Consolidating city services to the county level and moving remaining positions to voluntary roles.

78%
City expenses that are Labor & Benefits

With essential services moved to the county, remaining city positions (mayor, council, manager) become voluntary. By cutting 78% of labor costs, sales tax increases would be minimal — a well-run county could actually cut its tax rate.

City Bonds

Repeal all city bonding and pay off debts. Cities should not incur any debt.

City Vehicles

Return all city vehicles to eliminate this cost.
