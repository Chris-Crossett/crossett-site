County Reform

County Government

A shared services model to consolidate city functions and reduce spending.

Shared Services

Move Finance, Police, Fire, Road Crew, Parks, and Planning to the county level. Instead of 10 police chiefs for 10 cities, have 1. Cut workforce 50-60% while maintaining service levels.

Property Taxes

Repeal all property taxes. Fund with county portion of sales taxes only.

County Bonds

Repeal all county bonding and pay back all debts. Counties should not incur debt.
