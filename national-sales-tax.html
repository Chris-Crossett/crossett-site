Tax Reform

National Sales Tax

One tax. One rate. No loopholes. A hard ceiling to government spending.

The Concept
Replace ALL federal taxes with a single 10% National Sales Tax on consumer purchases. Groceries are exempt. No program survives without revenue — the 10% NST creates a hard ceiling to government spending.
~$20T
U.S. Personal Consumption (BEA, Q4 2025)
~$18T
Taxable base (minus ~$2T groceries)
~$1.8T
Annual revenue at 10%
  • Eliminates all federal taxes: income, payroll, corporate, excise, and estate/gift
  • Tax collected at point of sale — no individual filing required
  • Does not give favorability to one industry or business over another
  • All groceries exempt to protect families
