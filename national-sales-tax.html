National Sales Tax

A 10% national sales tax replacing all federal taxes — creating a hard ceiling to government spending.

Concept
Replace ALL federal taxes with a single 10% National Sales Tax on consumer purchases (groceries exempt). One tax. One rate. No loopholes. No program survives without revenue — the 10% NST provides a hard ceiling to spending.

Revenue Calculation
~$20T in annual U.S. personal consumption expenditures (BEA, Q4 2025), minus ~$2T in exempt grocery/food-at-home spending = ~$18T taxable base. At 10%, this generates approximately $1.8T annually.

Advantages

  • Eliminates all federal taxes: income tax, payroll tax, corporate tax, excise tax, estate/gift tax
  • Simple process to calculate and file returns
  • Does not give favorability to one industry or business over another
  • Exempt all Groceries (Food & Beverages) to protect families
  • IRS dramatically reduced or eliminated
  • No individual tax filing — tax is collected at point of sale
