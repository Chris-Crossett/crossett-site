State Reform

State Government

Fundamental reforms to achieve balanced budgets funded by sales tax revenue alone.

Create a Budget

Fund the entire state government from current sales tax revenues.

Health Care

Not the state's responsibility. Cut health care administration costs to zero.

Education

Sell all state colleges and universities to private entities for better competition on pricing and quality.

Transportation & Public Safety

Combine state police with county departments. State DOT receives doubled budget from combined fuel taxes.

DMV Reform

Registration only on transfer of ownership. Replace license plates with GPS devices under the hood.

Courts

Process nearly all cases online where judges review and rule electronically.

State Bonds

Repeal all state bonding. States should not incur debt.

State Taxes

Repeal all taxes except Motor Fuel and a fixed 7.5% Sales Tax.
