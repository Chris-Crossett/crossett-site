About Me

Fiscal conservative. Accounting & Finance professional. Problem solver.

I am a fiscal conservative. I am a strong believer in balanced budgets, especially for governments. My undergraduate degree is in Accounting, with a Masters in Business Administration.

Early on in my life, I was able to attend Town Halls in Vermont. This was quite a treat and I gained interest in how governments work. This also caused me to want to find solutions to the problems.

I exercise daily and as I do, I take time to critically think about the problems and what the solutions might be. Using this website helps me take my thoughts and "put them on paper" so that I can further my ideas and start to implement solutions.
