Government Reform

Privatization

Federal assets and programs to sell or transfer to reduce expenses and generate revenue.

National Parks / US Forest Service

Sell to businesses or states. Businesses can run parks more efficiently with access to capital for improvements.

United States Postal Service

Sell the USPS. FedEx, UPS, and others already do a fine job.

Bureau of Land Management

Sell all 247.3 million acres of BLM land in 12 western states.

Lending Programs

Sell all Student Loans, Home Loans, and SBA Loans. The federal government should not be in the lending business.

Department of Education

Eliminate the $70 billion expense. Education should be managed by the states. Commerce, Energy, IRS, and EPA can be reduced 50-90%.

Foreign Military

Either charge foreign governments 21.5% for services provided, or sell bases to local governments.

Transportation / Fuel Tax

Combine fuel taxes and add to state tax. States manage their own roads.

Airports & Trains

Sell airports and Amtrak. Maintain a small FAA for safety oversight.

Government Buildings

Sell buildings from closing departments, or sell and lease back.

Education Format

Move learning to tablet devices. Video lectures by top educators per subject per county. Electronic homework and testing. Students learn at their own pace.
