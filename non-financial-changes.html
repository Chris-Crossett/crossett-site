Structural Reform

Non-Financial Changes

Critical structural reforms beyond the budget.

Term Limits

Cap at 5 terms (10 years) for the House and 2 terms (12 years) for the Senate. Fresh ideas require fresh people.

U.S. Mint

  • Embrace Cryptocurrencies as legal tender
  • Cease making $1 and $5 bills — replace with coins
  • Cease making the 1 cent coin
  • Create a "coin card" smart card for change

Federal Reserve

  • Eliminate the Federal Reserve; use assets to pay down debt
  • Adopt Cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin for monetary supply

Homeland Security / CIA / FBI

Combine all three into one entity under the Defense Department, reducing duplicated jobs.

Repeal Tax Code

  • Remove limits on Retirement contributions
  • Remove limits on Health Savings Accounts
  • Repeal anything paid "after tax"
